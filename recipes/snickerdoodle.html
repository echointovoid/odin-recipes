Snickerdoodle Bars

Description:

These easy-to-make snickerdoodle bars have all the charm of their namesake cookies, but without all the hassle of rolling out the dough.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, divided
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line an 8x8 square pan with enough parchment paper to have overhang on the sides
  2. Cream together cutter, 1/2 cup white sugar, and brown sugar in a bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Mix in egg and vanilla until thoroughly combined. Add in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, salt, 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon, and nutmeg; mix until a dough just begins to form
  3. Place dough into prepared pan. Using lightly floured hands, press dough firmly and evenly into the bottom of the pan.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together 1 tablespoon white sugar and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon. Sprinkly evenly over the dough
  5. Place pan into the preheated oven, and bake just until the dough in the center of the pan feels barely set, 20-23 minutes. Do not over bake!
  6. Allow cookies to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely; cut into 16 bars
