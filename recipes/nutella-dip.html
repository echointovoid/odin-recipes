Midnight Nutella Dip

Description:

A go-to sweet late night snack that is quick and simple. Recommended to go with soft pretzels and milk.

Ingredients:

  • 5 tablespoons Nutella
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:

  1. Combine Nutella, milk, honey, and vanilla extract in a small microwave-safe container
  2. Heat in microwave for one minute
  3. Stir the dip with a toothpick until somewhat smooth; let it cool for two minutes before eating
