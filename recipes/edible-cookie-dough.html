Edible Cookie Dough

Description:

This edible cookie dough is egg-free and will satisfy any cravings for chocolate chip cookies without the wait.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. To heat-treat your flour so it is safe to use: Place flour in a microwave-safe dish and cook for one minute and fifteen seconds, stirring it every fifteen seconds; set aside
  2. Beat sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a large bowl until creamy. Beat in vanilla extract and salt. Add heat-treated flour; mix until a crumbly dough forms
  3. Stir in milk until dough is just combines; fold in milk chocolate chips and mini chocolate chips
